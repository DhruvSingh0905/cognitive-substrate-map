⚠ Gaps to verify before we build

This is the part to check — possible systems we have thin or missing. If "we get this right everything downstream becomes easier," these are the decisions:
  • Arousal / wake systems not yet in: adenosine (ADORA1, ADORA2A — the caffeine axis), histaminergic (HRH1/3), orexin (HCRT, HCRTR1/2). Relevant if "cognition" includes alertness.
  • Noradrenergic is absent — attention/arousal: ADRA2A, DBH, SLC6A2. Probably should be in.
  • Synaptic scaffolding absent: DLG4 (PSD-95), SHANK3, NLGN/NRXN — the structural side of plasticity.
  • Glutamate clearance: SLC1A2 (EAAT2) — excitotoxicity guardrail.
  • Neurotrophic beyond BDNF: NGF, GDNF.
  • Endocannabinoid: CNR1 — broad neuromodulator.
  • Epigenetic regulators: HDAC2, DNMT3A, TET1 — memory-relevant, but high-pleiotropy (likely lean toward trap).
  • Scope question: is the goal long-term plasticity/neuroprotection (current lean), or does it include acute performance (which pulls adenosine/noradrenergic/histaminergic to the center)?
Expansion (step 02) will pull in 1-hop PPI partners automatically, so a missing gene that's wired to a seed may still appear — but seeds are guaranteed in, so anything you consider canonical should be a seed, not left to expansion.
